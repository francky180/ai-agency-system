# Client Acquisition System

> Complete playbook for acquiring clients through the AI Agency System website.

---

## 1. Ideal Client Profile (ICP)

### Primary Target
- **Who:** Freelancers, solopreneurs, consultants, and small agency owners (1-5 people)
- **Revenue:** $0-$50K/year in services, looking to scale to $100K+
- **Pain:** Spending too much time on manual operations, can't afford a full team, struggling to systematize and scale
- **Desire:** Launch a professional AI-powered agency quickly without the $10K+ investment in custom development
- **Tech Level:** Basic to intermediate. Can follow instructions, not necessarily a developer

### Secondary Target
- **Who:** Existing agencies or businesses wanting AI infrastructure
- **Revenue:** $50K-$500K/year
- **Pain:** Know they need AI integration but don't know where to start, overwhelmed by options
- **Desire:** A done-for-you system with support — the $1,500 tier
- **Entry Point:** Strategy call or direct $1,500 purchase

### Disqualifiers
- Looking for a free solution
- No intention to actually run a business
- Expecting fully automated income with zero effort

## 2. Offer Positioning

### Core System ($250) — The Entry Point
- **Position as:** The lowest-risk way to launch an AI agency
- **Frame:** "Everything you need to go from zero to live agency in 30 minutes, for less than a single freelancer invoice"
- **Objection killer:** One-time payment, no subscriptions, no upsell traps
- **Comparison anchor:** "$12,000+ if you built this yourself"

### Full System ($1,500) — The Premium Upgrade
- **Position as:** The done-for-you path for serious operators
- **Frame:** "We build it, configure it, and launch it for you. You focus on closing clients"
- **Objection killer:** Includes priority support, custom setup, deployment assistance
- **Upsell from $250:** After purchase, follow up with "Ready to go further? We can do it all for you"

### Strategy Call — The High-Touch Path
- **Position as:** Free working session (not a sales pitch)
- **Frame:** "We map your goals and build a custom plan. If we're a fit, we'll talk about next steps"
- **Conversion target:** Upsell to $1,500 tier or custom engagement

## 9. Follow-Up System

### After Cold DM (No Response)
- **Day 2:** Soft bump ("Just making sure you saw this")
- **Day 7:** Value add (share a relevant tip or insight, no pitch)
- **Day 14:** Final touch ("No worries if not a fit. Here if you need it")
- After 3 touches with no response: move on

### After Cold Email (No Response)
- **Day 3:** Follow-up email (see script above)
- **Day 7:** New angle/subject line
- **Day 14:** Breakup email ("Last one from me — here if you need it")

### After Website Visit (No Purchase)
- If you have their email (lead magnet): trigger 3-email sequence
  - **Email 1 (Day 0):** Deliver the lead magnet + soft CTA
  - **Email 2 (Day 3):** Case study or results story + $250 CTA
  - **Email 3 (Day 7):** Urgency or scarcity angle + both offers

### After $250 Purchase
- **Day 0:** Automated onboarding email with access + quick-start guide
- **Day 3:** Check-in email ("How's the setup going? Need help?")
- **Day 7:** Upsell email ("Ready for the full system? Here's what you get with the $1,500 upgrade")
- **Day 14:** Referral ask ("Know anyone who'd benefit? Send them our way")

### After $1,500 Purchase
- **Day 0:** Personal welcome email + onboarding call scheduling
- **Day 7:** Check-in ("Everything running smoothly?")
- **Day 30:** Results check-in + testimonial ask
- **Day 60:** Referral ask + case study offer

### After Strategy Call (No Close)
- **Day 1:** Follow-up email with call summary + proposal
- **Day 3:** "Any questions about the proposal?"
- **Day 7:** Value add (relevant insight or resource)
- **Day 14:** Final touch with limited-time offer

## 10. Weekly KPIs
